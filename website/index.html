Chamilleia Supply & Demand

A TradingView indicator that highlights high-probability supply & demand setups — zones born from a Break of Structure, confirmed by a tap, an A+ wick, and a second lower-high / higher-low reversal.

What it marks on your chart

📈

Trend filter

EMA slope keeps you strictly on buys or strictly on sells — your daily bias, enforced.

🧱

Break of Structure

Detects breaks of the most recent swing high/low — valid by body or wick.

🟦

Auto zones

Draws the most recent opposite candle before the push, wick-to-wick, newest only (recency rule).

🎯

Tap & entry

BUY/SELL markers on the break-of-candle entry after a clean tap.

⭐

A+ setups

Stars the cleanest plays: price only wicks the zone, no body closes inside.

🪜

Minor BOS reversal

Confirms the stair-step flip with a second lower high (sells) / higher low (buys).

The 4-step core process

  1. 1

    Find the trend

    Clear bullish or bearish direction on the 5-minute. No trend → use S/R instead.

  2. 2

    Wait for a Break of Structure

    Price breaks a previous major swing high (buys) or low (sells). Body or wick both count.

  3. 3

    Wait for the tap

    The zone is a train stop. Price must fully pull back and tap it. No tap, no trade.

  4. 4

    Take the entry

    Aggressive, conservative (close out of zone), or break-of-candle. Stop outside the zone, target 1:1 then runner.

The full ruleset — invalidations, A+ criteria, minor BOS, support/resistance and counter trend-line breaks — lives in STRATEGY.md.

Install on TradingView

  1. Open TradingView → Pine Editor.
  2. Copy the contents of indicator/ChamilleiaSupplyDemand.pine.
  3. Paste → Save → Add to chart.
  4. Set the chart to the 5-minute timeframe.
  5. Tune settings & create alerts as needed.
